This Cookie Policy explains how andreweasyandereson.com uses cookies and similar technologies.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function and can provide information to the site owner.

How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies. Needed for the site to function properly.
  • Analytics cookies. Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. These are used only with your consent where required.

Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how the site works. For consent management, we use iQCookie.com.
