Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how andreweasyandereson.com uses cookies and similar browser storage, and how you can control them. It works alongside our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website can store in your browser. Websites also use related technologies, such as local storage, to remember information between visits. We use both sparingly and only as described below.

Our cookie consent system

On your first visit, a consent banner appears at the bottom of the screen. It briefly explains that we use cookies and links to this policy, and it gives you two clear choices: Accept or Decline. The banner is built in plain JavaScript with no third-party libraries, and it is keyboard accessible.

When you choose, we record your decision in your browser's local storage under the key iq_cookie_consent (with a value of "accepted" or "declined"). Because your choice is remembered, the banner does not appear again on future visits unless you clear it.

What we store

  • iq_cookie_consent (essential). Stored in local storage to remember whether you accepted or declined non-essential cookies. This is required for the consent system to work and is set as soon as you make a choice.
  • Analytics and performance (optional). If analytics or performance measurement are used on the site, they are only enabled after you click Accept. If you Decline, they are not loaded.

We do not use cookies to sell your data or to build advertising profiles about you.

How to withdraw or change your consent

You can withdraw or change your consent at any time:

  • Reset your choice on this site. Clear this site's data in your browser (or specifically remove the iq_cookie_consent entry from local storage). The next time you load the site, the consent banner will appear again so you can choose differently.
  • Use your browser settings. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies and storage for individual sites or for all sites. Refer to your browser's help pages for instructions.

Note that blocking essential storage may affect how the consent banner behaves, since your choice can no longer be remembered.

Third-party properties

When you follow a link to one of our other properties (such as iQCookie.com, AEOWebsiteChecker.com, or iQ Marketers) or to any external site, that site sets its own cookies under its own policy. This Cookie Policy covers only andreweasyandereson.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above.
