Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Andrew "Easy" Anderson and iQ Marketers are committed to making andreweasyandereson.com accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. Accessibility is built into how this site is designed, not added afterward.

Conformance status

This site is designed and built to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities.

What we do

  • Color contrast. Text and meaningful interface elements meet or exceed WCAG 2.2 AA contrast ratios (at least 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text and user-interface components).
  • Minimum 18px font size. Body and interface text is at least 18px everywhere on the site, with no exceptions, for comfortable reading.
  • Keyboard navigation. All interactive elements — links, buttons, the contact form, and the cookie consent banner — can be reached and operated with a keyboard alone.
  • Skip link. Every page begins with a "Skip to content" link so keyboard and screen-reader users can bypass the navigation.
  • Visible focus. A clear focus indicator shows which element is selected as you move through the page.
  • Semantic structure. Pages use proper landmarks and a single, logical heading order so assistive technology can navigate them.
  • Descriptive text. Images include alternative text, links use meaningful labels, and form fields are clearly labelled.
  • Reduced motion. We respect the "reduce motion" setting in your operating system and turn off non-essential animation.
  • No italics. Body copy avoids italic type, which can be harder to read for some people.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing commitment. We test with keyboard navigation and automated checks, and we continue to review and improve the site over time. Some third-party content or external sites we link to may not meet the same standards, and those are outside our direct control.

Reporting an accessibility issue

If you encounter a barrier on this site, we want to fix it. Please tell us what happened, the page you were on, and the browser or assistive technology you were using. Contact us at hello@andreweasyandereson.com, use the contact form, or write to iQ Marketers, 15551 Old Hickory Blvd, Suite A. We aim to respond promptly and to resolve issues as quickly as we can.
