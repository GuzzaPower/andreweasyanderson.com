Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Andrew "Easy" Anderson is committed to ensuring this website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

Conformance status

This site aims to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities.

Measures we take

  • Semantic HTML and clear heading structure for screen readers.
  • Text and interface colors that meet AA contrast ratios.
  • A minimum font size of 18px throughout the site.
  • Visible keyboard focus indicators and a skip-to-content link.
  • Respect for reduced-motion preferences.
  • Descriptive link text and labelled form controls.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this site. If you encounter a barrier, please contact us at hello@andreweasyandereson.com and we will work to resolve it.
